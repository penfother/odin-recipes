Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possible one of the oldest types, made of a very wide, flat sheets. Lasagna can also refer to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such as ragu, bechamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses and seasonings and spices.
